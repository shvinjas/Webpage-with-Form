Full Stack Development involves creating web applications from front-end to back-end. Their role includes designing user interfaces, implementing features, and managing the entire development process. This versatility is particularly valuable in dynamic environments such as startups, where Full Stack Developers contribute across multiple facets of a project.

Full Stack Developers employ a range of key technologies and tools to build comprehensive web applications. On the front end, they utilize HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, often leveraging popular frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js. For server-side scripting, languages like Node.js, Python, or Ruby are commonly used, along with frameworks like Express (Node.js) or Django (Python). Database management involves technologies like MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL.
